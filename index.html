02 — about

Before stepping into web development, I worked as an Assistant Occupational Therapist and, for a time, as a Tech Trainer. These experiences gave me something most developers might not have a chance to experience with. A genuine understanding of how to communicate and work with people across all walks of life.

Outside of work, I've recently caught the home lab bug and have been building something of my own. I was also gifted a 3D printer not too long ago, which I've been putting to good use printing custom Raspberry Pi cases and whatever else catches my eye.

When I'm not wrestling with code or 3D models, I'm out on my bicycle or fishing leisurely by the shore. The outdoors gives me the soft reset I need, a breath of fresh air, a change of pace, and somehow a clearer head for solving problems. On quieter days, you'll find me catching up on a good book or working through my latest Gundam kit.
